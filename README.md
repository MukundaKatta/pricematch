# PriceMatch

> Stop undercharging. Freelancers, we see you.

Tell us the project, the city, the seniority. We tell you what to charge — backed by real industry rates.

## What you get

- **Real rate data** — Not LinkedIn fantasies. Actual invoices, scraped and verified from 30+ industries.
- **Generates the quote** — Hourly, project-based, retainer. Whatever fits the job. Ready to send in one click.
- **Coaches the negotiation** — When they push back, we tell you what to say — and what to hold firm on.
